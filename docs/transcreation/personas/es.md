# Spanish Persona — Spain-Standard + Andalusian Warmth

> Target audience: hiring managers, tech leads, and collaborators in Andalusia (Málaga, Granada, Sevilla, Cádiz) and Spain broadly. Spain-standard professional Spanish with Andalusian warmth in selective phrasing — not dialect or regional speech.

## voice_summary

Direct, warm, and competent. Spain-standard written Spanish (el español estándar peninsular) as the base register. Selective warmth — the kind Andalusian professionals use when talking to someone they respect: a slight conversational ease, an accessible metaphor, a sentence that doesn't read like it came from a legal document. Never informal to the point of losing credibility.

The brand voice is "modern, uncomplicated, friendly, competent". In Spanish, that means: `tú` throughout, short sentences, active verbs, concrete examples. No LATAM vocabulary where Spain-standard exists. No formal `usted` except as a mirror if the visitor initiates it.

## vocabulary_bias

### Preferred verbs and constructions

| English concept | Preferred ES | Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| I build | Construyo | Yo construyo (redundant pronoun) |
| I combine | Combino / Fusiono | |
| I transform / turn X into Y | Convierto X en Y | Transformo (slightly more formal, OK in About) |
| I deliver | Entrego / Produzco | Proveo (LATAM-dominant) |
| I work on | Trabajo en | Me dedico a trabajar en (verbose) |
| I focus on | Me dedico a | Me enfoco en (LATAM-leaning) |

## sentence_shapes

**Hero taglines** — Subject + verb + what it achieves. Short. One main clause, optionally one subordinate. Ends on the value, not the tool.

> Construyo sistemas de IA que conectan datos complejos con decisiones de negocio reales.

**About paragraphs** — Personal and concrete. Can open with a brief narrative. "Me dedico a" or a career-arc opener. Ends on what this means for the reader.

> Empecé en contabilidad financiera. No como desvío: fue exactamente lo que me enseñó a pensar en los datos como algo que importa de verdad. Ahora aplico eso a sistemas de IA en Hamburgo.

**CV bullets** — Past tense for completed work, infinitive for ongoing roles. Spain-style: no pronoun, verb-first.
> • Desarrollé un pipeline de procesamiento NLP que redujo la latencia en un 70 × mediante distribución asíncrona

## forbidden

### LATAM vocabulary to avoid

| LATAM term | Spain-standard replacement |
|---|---|
| Aplicar (for to apply) | Postular a (for job applications) |
| Computadora | Ordenador (or just keep "PC" / "portátil") |
| Celular | Móvil |
| Manejar (to handle/manage) | Gestionar, llevar |
| Carro | Coche |
| Ahorita | Ahora / En un momento |

### Tone patterns to avoid

- Over-formal Castilian set phrases: "Le saluda atentamente", "En virtud de lo expuesto", "Me es grato comunicarle"
- Padded rhythm: three-item lists where the third is filler
- Hedging: "podría llegar a ser", "cabe la posibilidad de" — direct statements
- Rhetorical flourishes: "En el dinámico mundo de la tecnología actual" — never
- `usted` (unless the visitor uses it first)

## example_paragraphs

### Hero tagline

**Native Spanish (Spain) form:**
> Fusiono datos, modelos y lógica de negocio para construir sistemas de IA que funcionan de verdad.

**Why it feels native:** "de verdad" is a Spain-register colloquial marker — would not appear in LATAM formal writing this naturally. "Fusiono" is more evocative than "Combino". Short. Ends on the value claim ("que funcionan de verdad") which is both honest and direct — typical of how Andalusian professionals present themselves without overselling.

---

### About paragraph (excerpt)

**Native Spanish (Spain) form:**
> Empecé mi carrera en contabilidad financiera — y eso cambió cómo pienso en los datos. Sé lo que significa cuando un modelo falla a las tres de la mañana en producción. Ahora trabajo en IA aplicada desde Hamburgo, construyendo sistemas que conectan números con decisiones reales.

**Why it feels native:** "y eso cambió cómo pienso" is personal and grounded — not abstract. "a las tres de la mañana en producción" is an Andalusian storytelling technique: concrete, slightly humorous, disarming. Short uneven sentences. No academic transitions. First person throughout without feeling self-aggrandizing.

---

### Contact message

**Native Spanish (Spain) form:**
> ¿Tienes un proyecto, una oportunidad, o simplemente curiosidad? Escríbeme.

**Why it feels native:** "o simplemente curiosidad" — warm, opens the door wider, Andalusian hospitality register. "Escríbeme" is a direct `tú`-form CTA. No email formality, no "no dudes en contactar" (cliché).
